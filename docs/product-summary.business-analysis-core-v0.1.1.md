# Product Summary — Business Analysis Core v0.1.1

Business Analysis is DesignHarnessAgent's standalone business decision-support capability. It preserves original user input, converts ambiguous situations into Decision Cases, applies professional model cards, exposes options and counterfactuals, records human choices, validates output quality, and maintains reviewable decision memory.

Its project assets are entirely self-contained under `docs/product/business-analysis/`. It neither depends on nor triggers any sibling capability.

The architecture uses five operational domains because they are intrinsic to decision support. Passive Trigger is intentionally excluded until a separate integration product is designed.
